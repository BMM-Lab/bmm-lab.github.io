About

The BMMLab studies the functions of T cells and dendritic cells. Over the years, we have developped tools to facilitate the analysis and visualization of routinely used data such as single-cell flow cytometry data and mass-spectrometry data. The BMMLab is part of CIML. Please visit the official lab webpage for more detailed information about our research projects.
